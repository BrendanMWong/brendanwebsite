Hunger Games Simulator

This is a text- and image-based simulation that allows users to recreate the Hunger Games in their browser. It served two key purposes: it provided entertainment to the users who tried it, and it helped me earn about $190 while utilizing it in a YouTube video.

The Hunger Games is a popular trilogy of novels written by Suzanne Collins, where contestents fight in a large arena full of treacherous terrain, until only one is left standing. Inspiration for this project began while I was playing with a Hunger Game simulator hosted at brantsteele.net.
